### **Exercise 1: Dynamics Practice**
*Learn to control volume and tone*

### **Exercise 2: Legato vs Staccato**
**Legato** (smooth, overlapping):
```
Hold each key as you press the next:
**111**222**444**555**666**
```

**Staccato** (short, separated):
```
Quick taps with space between:
1 -- 2 -- 4 -- 5 -- 6 --
```

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Realistic Piano Sounds

### **1. Use the Natural Decay** âœ¨
- Notes fade over ~1.2 seconds
- You can play overlapping melodies
- Let chords ring out beautifully

### **2. Timing is Everything** â±ï¸
- **Fast songs**: Quick taps, let decay separate notes
- **Slow songs**: Hold keys longer for smooth legato
- **Rhythmic songs**: Match the beat with your presses

### **3. Experiment with Sharps** ğŸµ
- Shift+1,2,4,5,6,8,9 add color and emotion
- Use sharps for jazzy, bluesy, or mysterious sounds
- Try replacing natural notes with sharps for variation

### **4. Play with Expression** ğŸ’«
- Vary your timing for musicality
- Hold important notes longer
- Add pauses for dramatic effect

### **5. Practice Makes Perfect** ğŸ¯
- Start SLOW - speed comes with muscle memory
- Practice one hand patterns first
- Listen to how notes blend together
